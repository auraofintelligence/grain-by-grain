## 0. North star

One ladder from the Gumpi jetty to a civilisation that keeps the lights on through anything: climate shocks, Carrington-class storms, and worse. Kardashev Type 1 capability and quasi-infinite civilisation longevity, held inside Joyful Responsible Abundance.

Operating principles:

- No waiting for authority to dream. No asking for money to build. Momentum, step by step.
- Every rung runs in two lanes. The Build lane is what can be done now: evidence, bench tests, skills, working software. The Screen lane is where a rung goes when cost, law or cultural authority blocks physical work: simulation, digital twin, cinema. Nothing gets binned; it gets staged.
- Authority is not defied, it is replaced by reproducibility. Claims earn standing by becoming runnable models tested against shared data (the Sensorium method). Anyone can run the same model against the same numbers.
- Minjerribah is Quandamooka Country. This project claims no cultural authority and implies no endorsement. QYAC is the doorway for land and sea decisions. Any real work is held to the standard of the best agreements on record (WGEH Mirning equity, Noongar Boodja Trust).
- The spine wires the existing chapter sites together; it does not duplicate them. Chapters stay live at their own URLs.

## 2. Register system

Five evidence labels. Every claim-bearing card, panel or number carries exactly one, rendered as a stamped chip:

- `KNOWN` - sourced fact or operating precedent (Polar Night sand battery, Mutriku OWC, 2019 mining cessation).
- `MODELLED` - runnable simulation or engineering calculation exists or is planned (MODFLOW aquifer twin, mass/energy balances).
- `DISPUTED` - live academic contest (Younger Dryas impact debate).
- `SPECULATIVE` - author synthesis, physics-permitting, nobody has built it (lateral wall mining as habitable-void creation).
- `FICTIONAL` - cinematic canon (micro-nova, Sub-Oceanic Technate, Cosmic Nexus).

Rules:

1. Labels replace disclaimer paragraphs. The honesty lives in the chip so the copy can be bold. No anxious repetition.
2. Aspirational numbers are marked as aspiration ("1 km/week is the aspiration marker"), never presented as estimates.
3. Fringe and mainstream compete in the same sandbox: to move up a label, a claim becomes a runnable model tested against shared data.
4. Threat-agnostic hardening: engineer for the consensus-priced threat (Carrington-class storm); let cinema explore the deeper ones. Nobody has to win the argument for the work to proceed.

Two lanes, colour-coded site-wide: Build lane (high-vis orange), Screen lane (projector violet).
