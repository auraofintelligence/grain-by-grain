Site map & growth plan

This site is built to grow toward hundreds of pages, bringing together and improving the engineering and simulation work now scattered across the constellation. So this map shows more than what's built: it shows the surveyed ground each rung will grow into, and marks honestly what is live, what is under construction, and what is only named so far. Nothing here is a "coming soon" tease. It's a plan you can watch fill in.
