## 1. North star

One ladder from the Gumpi jetty to a civilisation that keeps the lights on through anything, up to and including the deep-time threats. Kardashev Type 1 capability and quasi-infinite civilisation longevity, held inside Joyful Responsible Abundance.

This repo ASSIMILATES and UPGRADES all the civil-engineering and simulation content of the constellation (the full roster is §4), at the density of the hi-def source docs, growing toward hundreds of pages in staged waves. It does NOT absorb the grounded evidence/community chapters: gumpi ferry lab, ballow-road and ready-set-co-op stay standalone, linked from their rungs. Old repos stay live until superseded; links back are temporary; never a big-bang cutover.

Principles:
- No waiting for authority to dream. No asking for money to build. Momentum, step by step.
- Two lanes at every rung: Build (evidence, bench, skills, software, agreements) and Screen (twin, simulation, film). Blocked work gets staged, never binned.
- Authority is replaced by reproducibility: claims become runnable models anyone can re-run (the Sensorium method).
- Minjerribah is Quandamooka Country. No cultural authority claimed or implied; QYAC is the doorway; real work is held to the best agreements on record (WGEH Mirning equity, Noongar Boodja Trust).

## 3. The rules (distilled, permanent)

**AUDIENCE & VOICE (read first, applies to every word on every front-facing page. Luke should never have to repeat this.)**
- WHO IT'S FOR: everyday middle-class Australians and Quandamooka/Indigenous readers. No academic vocabulary, not developers, not lawyers, not WH&S officers, not Luke, not the AI. If a sentence would only make sense to someone who has read the source docs or built software, it is wrong. Plain words, short, warm, resonant with a real person's journey. Gloss or avoid every technical term; lead numbers with a mind's-eye analogy.
- NO BACKSTAGE / META TALK, EVER. Never mention (on a page) the corpus, the source documents, "our research", drafts, a verification pass, how the page was made, or that an AI was involved. Never write "this page", "this ladder", "this site does X", "the page keeps only what survives", "what this page owes you". Work ideas out in side .md files, never in front-facing copy. The reader is on a journey; they do not want to watch the sausage get made.
- NO CHIP-MACHINERY NARRATION. The small KNOWN/MODELLED/DISPUTED/SPECULATIVE/FICTIONAL tags stay. But NEVER write prose that explains or narrates them: no "wears the/its chip", "wears its honest label", "Honest status:", "Modelled: ...", "the register chips are the deal", "the label moved not the claim". Say the honest thing in plain words ("this isn't built yet") and let the little tag do its job silently.
- HONEST TENSE (critical). Almost nothing here is built; it is theory, logic and dreaming in public. NEVER state planned island infrastructure as current or completed. Banned on unbuilt things: "already", "live", "running", "active", "now", "exists", past-tense "we built/proved/showed/ran", and bare present tense that asserts operation ("the microgrid runs the island", "the twin lets an elder stand there", "the loop closes"). Use the conditional/aspirational voice the site is built on: "would", "could", "the plan is", "one day", "a microgrid that would run alone". Rung status labels must never say "Active" for a page/idea that isn't a real running build. Genuinely-true real-world facts (existing precedents, real physics/chemistry, real statistics, existing chapter sites) may stay present-tense as facts.
- INSIDER TERMS get glossed on first use or avoided: "Build lane"/"Screen lane", "Sensorium", "spine", "workbenches", "atoms/modules/trajectories", "assimilate/corpus". A first-time reader must not hit an undefined codeword.
- TONE: honest exploration and dreaming in public so the path to action gets clearer and collaborative, following the proper pathways with agility and care. NOT authoritative declaration. No brandished absolutes (NEVER/ALWAYS/MANDATE/"unbreakable rule"); sensible bold joyful responsible abundance. Genuine safety/wildlife limits may stay firm.
- Australian English ALWAYS (organise not organize, honour not honor, colour, metre, etc.); only real proper names keep their own spelling. NEVER an em dash (use comma, colon, semicolon or full stop). These two are permanent and non-negotiable; do not wait to be reminded.

**Register**
- Five chips: KNOWN (sourced fact/operating precedent), MODELLED (runnable calc/sim exists or is planned), DISPUTED (live contest, say exactly what is argued), SPECULATIVE (physics permits, unbuilt/unproven), FICTIONAL (story artefacts and films ONLY).
- Natural-hazard hypotheses are NEVER chipped FICTIONAL. Mega-tsunami phenomenon is KNOWN (Lituya Bay 1958, Storegga, Hunga Tonga 2022 for the sub-ocean volcano trigger), timing DISPUTED. The solar micro-nova is SPECULATIVE + DISPUTED frontier science, ranked FIRST among threats by the founder, never downplayed; term credited to Ben Davidson and the frontier before astronomy adopted it in 2022; the Sensorium falsification invitation is the honest frame.
- Labels replace disclaimer paragraphs; copy stays bold. Aspirational numbers are marked as aspiration. Threat-agnostic hardening: build for the priced threats and the deep ones cost the argument nothing.

**Energy**
- NO SURFACE DISTURBANCE, ever. Roofs, structures, shorelines, swell; never cleared land. "Ground solar" is banned vocabulary.
- No wind turbines anywhere in the plan; no exposed blade or moving part that could injure wildlife. Enclosed/sealed turbines (OWC air ducts, pumped-hydro penstocks) are fine and the enclosure is stated explicitly.
- Not energy maximisation for profit: responsible harvest, everywhere, gradually, multi-generational.
- Pumped hydro is a first-class citizen (it was a big part of the clean-energy site); storage always shows honest round-trip losses; heat stores for heat needs, electricity stores for electricity needs.

**Water**
- The island USES its freshwater lens and always will (~20 ML/day already leaves it for Redlands, ~10x island use; receipts on walls.html). Desalination ADDS supply for industry, dry years and new demand; it never pretends to replace the lens. Aquifer twin (MODFLOW) before any machine.

**Materials**
- Thorium is a future fuel held in trust, not a nuisance: proven at Oak Ridge in the 1960s, dropped for being hard to weaponise; China's TMSR-LF1 bred fuel from thorium in 2025, India builds on monazite sands; AU ban is a moving wall. Always pair with the local history: these dunes were mined for monazite, abandoned in the 1990s because thorium made the REEs "too costly", dispersed back into tailings. The 20th-century waste is the 21st-century fuel.
- Reclaimed streams are the island's richest ore: e-waste, appliances, metals dumped only because no local system exists. The TIP LOOP (repair-and-reclaim at the tip) and MAKER-SPACE bench are the system, both being folded into this repo.
- Rare-earth magnets serve the island's motors, generators and bladeless mesh (never "wind turbines").
